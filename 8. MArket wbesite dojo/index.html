Frequently Asked Questions

How do I become a vendor on Trygone?
To become a vendor, simply sign up for an account and choose the vendor option during registration. You'll then be guided through the process of setting up your store.
What payment methods are accepted?
We accept a wide range of payment methods including credit cards, PayPal, and various cryptocurrencies to ensure convenience for all our users.
How does the review system work?
Our review system is blockchain-verified to ensure authenticity. Only customers who have made a purchase can leave a review, maintaining the integrity of our ratings.
